Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a parking spot?

Simply go to the Home page, select a parking spot from the map or list, and click "Reserve Now". You'll need to confirm your reservation and payment details.

Can I cancel a reservation?

Yes, you can cancel reservations up to 30 hour before the scheduled start time without any penalty. Go to "Active Sessions" to manage your bookings.

How do I become a parking host?

Go to "Add Parking" in the navigation menu, fill out the parking space details, and submit for verification. Once approved, you can start hosting!
